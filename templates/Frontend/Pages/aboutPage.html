St. John High School

St. John's High School is an English Medium School with a Boarding Facility. It was founded by the UBA(Udaipur Baptist Association) on the 20th August 1988, At Embru Panthor(Khakchang Hathi) near Killa. It was then shifted to Raio in 1991. This School was established with a view of educating the chidren of the interior villages of Tripura. It was upgraded to Senior Basic School in 1997 and to High School in 1999. It is recognized by the Department of Education-Govt. of Tripura and was affiliated to TBSE and later it was converted to CBSE 2015.
It also aims to give children a sound physical, intellectual, moral and spritual education based on Christian Principles.
This NGo School is run and managed by the UBA(Udaipur Baptist Association) whose prime aim and objectives are mentioned in the memorandum of UBA. And Thereforethe Principles aims and objectives of the UBA will be reflected and followed in the School.
