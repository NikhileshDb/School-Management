About Us

St.John High School is an English Medium School with a Boarding Facility. It was founded by the UBA(Udaipur Baptist Association) on the 20th Aug, 1988 at Embru Panthor(Khakchang Hati) near Killa. It was then shifted to Raio in 1991. It was upgraded to Senior Basic School 1997 and to a High School in 1999. It is recognized by the Department of Education-Govt. of Tripura and was affiliated to TBSE and later it was converted to CBSE in 2015.
