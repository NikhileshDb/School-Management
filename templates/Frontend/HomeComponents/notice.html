Principal's Message

Dear parents, students and well-wishers,

Welcome to Saint John’s High School website. As principal, I am impressed by your courtesy to go through our school website. Please remember, we, the management and the staff, are all set for an excellent all-round education of our beloved students at any cost.

Under my leadership, with my dedicated management, staffs, together with parents, strongly promote academic achievement among our students. We make special effort and emphasis on co-curricular/extra-curricular activities to identify students’ capabilities according to their talents.

In-fact, school is the second home of our students and teachers are the second parents (Gurus). With this strong bond of love for the students and responsibility towards them, we leave “No Stone Unturned” to provide excellent inclusive education and impart proper moral discipline.

We have NO any distinction among the students based on gender, caste, creed, ethnicity and religion etc but to treat them as the treasure and hope of future India. With a long and rewarding history of achievement in education, sports and culture behind us, our school continues to move forward with confidence, pride and enthusiasm.

I hope you enjoy your visit to our school website

Yours in educational service
(Kulandai Swami)
Principal
Saint John’s High School
Udaipur, Gomati Tripura.
